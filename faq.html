FAQ

Is MailGen free?

MailGen is free for up to 2000 subscribers in your list. Pricing depends on your list, please visit our Pricing Page for more information.

What will happen if I exceed my contacts limit?

Because MailGen does not require a credit card during registratio, we can not automatically upgrade your account, you need to do it manually.

What do I need to do in order to cancel my subscription?

In order to cancel your subscription, you can contact our support team or visit your account dashboard. Bear in mind that once you requested cancellation, it will take effect on the next day of payment.

Do you offer affiliate programs?

No. Currently we do not offer an affiliate program, but we're definetely working towards it!
